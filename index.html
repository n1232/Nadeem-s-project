What is Eating right?

We are a website which promotes healthy eating, something which is important due to the effects which diet can have on our weight, physique and even mental health. This is what why we have put together this website consisting of info on a balanced diet, healthy recipes and more in order to try and encourage others towards a healthier eating practise.
